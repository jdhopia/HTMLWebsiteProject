The place for all your favorite Celtic inspired items!

What is your return policy?

We allow returns up to 30 days after your package arrives as long as all clothing is unworn and kitchen ware is unused.

Do you ship outside the U.S.?

We currently do not ship outside of the U.S. We are still a fairly small business and are working to expand our shipping capabilities.

How long does it for my order to ship?

It takes us about 2-3 days to ship out the order and then it takes up to a week or two for the package to be delivered.

Do you offer more than one shipping option?

Yes, we offer normal shipping, expedited shipping, and a gift package where we wrap the gift for you.

Do the clothing items typically fit to size?

Yes, we have recieved a lot of feedback from customers that our sizing is very accurate to what you would expect.

How do I track my order?

After your purchase has gone through you should receive a confirmation email that contains a tracking number.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal.

There is a problem with my order, who do I contact about it?

You can contact us through email at CelticChicInfo@CelticChic.com.

Do you offer shipping insurance?

We do not offer shipping insurance, however most postal services do and you can insure it by providing them with your tracking number.
